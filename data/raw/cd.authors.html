Authors included in TLG disks D and E

Occasionally, author names are enclosed within angle brackets '<>' or square brackets '[]'. Angle brackets indicate an author to whom a given work has been assigned, although that person may not be the author of the work in question. Square brackets enclosing an author's name are intended to question the authenticity of the name; even the existence of the author may be disputed.
